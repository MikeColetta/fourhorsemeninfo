About

The Story of Four Horsemen.

  • 2015

    Four Horsemen Opens!

    Four Horsemen Brewery starts brewing locally crafted beer with 100% local Washington grown ingredients.

  • 2016

    We won!

    The brewery's Black Plague Stout won the “Best Washington Malt Beer Award” in 2016.

  • 2020

    Four Horsemen Concessions!

    Four Horsemen Brewery expanded to include a new entity: Four Horsemen Concessions. Four Horsemen Concessions is the exclusive provider of food and beverage services for Pacific Raceway's premier motorsports complex.

  • 2021

    Osiris Food Truck!

    Four Horsemen continues expansion and opens their new Food Truck: Osiris. With 53ft in length, Osiris is Washington State's largest food truck.

Our Amazing Team

Meet our team!

Dane Scarimbolo

Owner/CEO

Dane is one of the main founders of Four Horsemen Brewery. He attended Washington State University where he got my degree majoring in Viticulture and Enology. Dane also studied Landscape Architecture, Horticulture and Greenhouse Management. He has worked in many different wineries in the United States as well as abroad in Mendoza Argentina. Dane loves to make beer, cook, and create something people can enjoy. That hopefully leaves a lasting memorable impression where people want to come back!

Dominique Torgerson

Owner/COO

Dominique also graduated from Washington State with a degree in Criminal Justic. So completely unrelated to anything Brewery. In 2010 she joined what was initially her brother's dream, but ended up becoming Dominique's dream too to own a family business together. Dominique loves being part of something that creates positive memories for people. Having also worked in a multitude of customer service positions, she greatly enjoy being able to be part of making a memorable experience for someone.
